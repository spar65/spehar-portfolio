## 🚀 #1 - AI Assess Tech: Runtime Behavioral Governance for Deployed AI

**Description**: AI Assess Tech is the runtime behavioral governance and assessment platform for deployed AI systems. Where competitors focus on training-time alignment, AI Assess Tech measures what AI actually *does* once it's running in production — across four behavioral dimensions: Lying, Cheating, Stealing, and Harm (LCSH). Results are cryptographically verified using SHA-256 hash chains and anchored to the Ethereum blockchain, producing evidence that survives audit, regulator scrutiny, and customer due diligence.

**The Problem It Solves — The "Competent Psychopath":** Most AI today is evaluated on whether it produces correct outputs. But an AI can be technically correct and still be quietly harmful — denying loans unfairly, hiding errors, gaming metrics, drifting under load. We named this the "Competent Psychopath" problem: AI that executes tasks efficiently while making unfair or harmful decisions invisibly. Training-time alignment cannot detect it. Only runtime behavioral assessment can.

**Use Case:** Essential for enterprises deploying AI in regulated and high-stakes industries — insurance, financial services, healthcare, government, and any organization that needs to *prove* their deployed AI behaves the way they say it does.
