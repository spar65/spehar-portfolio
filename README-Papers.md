Through systematic investigation, empirical validation, and mathematical formalization, I aim to transform AI-assisted development and AI governance from experimental domains into reliable, measurable, and optimizable engineering disciplines.

Each paper contributes to a coherent research program that addresses fundamental questions about how humans and AI systems can collaborate effectively — and how we can *prove* deployed AI behaves the way we say it does. This is Phase 1: runtime behavioral assessment of software-based AI.
